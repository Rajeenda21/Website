The Las Vegas Grand Prix is a Formula One Grand Prix car racing event, which is part of the 2023 Formula One season. It takes place in Las Vegas, Nevada, United States, precisely on the Las Vegas Strip. The first race will be held on November 18, 2023.

Date: Sun, 26 Nov, 20.00
Track: Yas Marina Circuit

The Abu Dhabi Grand Prix (Arabic: سباق جائزة أبوظبي الكبرى) is a Formula One auto race. The Grand Prix was first announced in early 2007 at the Abu Dhabi F1 Festival in the United Arab Emirates. This race was first held on 1 November 2009 at the Yas Marina Circuit, which was designed by Hermann Tilke.
